## Screen Time Analytics - Synopsis

**Executive Summary:**

The Screen Time Analytics feature provides users with actionable insights into their digital habits, promoting digital wellness and productivity. By tracking application usage and categorizing time spent as productive or distracting, users can gain a clear understanding of how they allocate their time in the digital world.

**Key Benefits:**

*   **Increased Awareness:** Empowers users to understand their digital habits and identify areas for improvement.
*   **Improved Productivity:** Helps users minimize distractions and focus on productive activities.
*   **Data-Driven Insights:** Provides clear and concise visualizations of screen time data, including bar charts and pie charts.
*   **Personalized Recommendations:** Offers tailored suggestions for improving digital wellness based on individual usage patterns.

**Target Audience:**

*   Students seeking to improve their study habits.
*   Professionals aiming to enhance their productivity.
*   Individuals interested in promoting their overall digital wellness.

**Technical Highlights:**

*   Utilizes a robust data model to capture detailed screen time information.
*   Provides a RESTful API for seamless data retrieval.
*   Employs the `react-chartjs-2` library to create interactive and visually appealing charts.

**Value Proposition:**

The Screen Time Analytics feature enables users to take control of their digital lives, leading to increased productivity, improved focus, and enhanced overall well-being. This feature is a valuable addition to our application, demonstrating our commitment to providing users with the tools they need to succeed in the digital age.
